History

Spanish History

Here you can fing more information about the Spanish culture

Link to Information

From Spain we have wanted to bring the extensive cultural tradition of our country to another as different as Romania.

The business was founded in 1990 in Valencia, Spain. But it was not until 2020 that it was decided to internationalize the company.

In order to make the traditional Spanish cuisine as similar as possible, the place is decorated in pure Spanish style so that the customer's experience is as close as possible.

Team

The El Olé restaurant team is made up of some of the most famous Spanish chefs in the area, each one specialized in one of the country's typical dishes.
